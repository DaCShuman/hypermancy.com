Species of the Realms

Paths of Power seeks to present an intriguing and fresh take on species and their interactions across the Realms.
Any species develops genetic advantages and disadvantages based on their history of evolution. A people from a planet with extreme heat or cold climates will be very adept at handling those environments and less capable in the opposite. Other advantages might present themselves, such as a species that developed on a densely forested planet with a large quantity of predatory flora and fauna will naturally have developed stealthier habits and mannerisms. Species in PoP, present with advantages and disadvantages that figure into the Mods, when applicable. During Character Creation these are figured in as Evolution Points (EP) and will account for social and genetic advantages and disadvantages.

A Core Principle

To be absolutely clear, Hypermancy Studios does not tolerate or condone bigotry, racism, slavery, or any ideology that claims the superiority of one being above another. These forms of ignorance have no place in our game worlds or our community.
Every species and lineage in our setting is designed to enrich storytelling, celebrate diversity, and explore unique strengths and challenges. Crucially, no species is inherently superior. Differences exist solely to grant unique narrative pathways and mechanical choices, never to reinforce harmful real-world prejudices.
