Hypermancy Studios: Our Mission & Philosophy

Our Core Mission

Hypermancy Studios empowers storytellers to craft bigger, braver stories. Through the open-licensed Magnitude Dice System (MDS), we enhance player agency with a story-first toolkit that never neglects the potential for epic power. We design tools that turn imagination into action and help stories evolve from the ordinary to the extraordinary.

Our Values and Promise

We are guided by the values of Creativity, Accessibility, and Respect.

We commit to stewarding a community where storytelling thrives and ethical design endures.

We will always honor player ingenuity and equip Game Masters with robust tools to govern their tables and tell their stories with confidence and clarity.

The Magnitude Dice System

The Magnitude Dice System is a story-first, power-not-neglected engine. It equips creators with open and accessible mechanics designed to measure and amplify the extraordinary, ensuring storytelling is always empowered, never restricted.

Paths of Power

Our flagship setting, Paths of Power, embodies a Nobledark in essence.
 Dark - The universe is brutal. Life is hard. Everyone has been suffering across the Realms for millennia.
 Noble - Your characters impact the universe. Heroes can, and will do great things that will impact the universe.
We are beings of hope, and in a universe drawn apart, we dream of bringing everything back together. These are the Paths of Power that heroes walk.

About Us

We are designers dedicated to uncapping true potential in every story. Hypermancy Studios builds powerful, accessible tools that empower players to shape the cosmos and transform their narratives from the ordinary to the epic.
