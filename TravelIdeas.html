Traversal & Transportation

How travelers cross cities, worlds, and realms in the Paths of Power universe.

Overview

Movement in PoP ranges from the mundane to the mythical. Whether you’re booking passage on a skyship, stepping through a gate keyed to a distant city, or surfing a ley-line current between Living Worlds, each method has its own costs, risks, and requirements. As with everything in MDS, Traits set your dice pools, Talents shape ease and options, and Mods reflect the Situation: weather, crowds, security, Gusts, and more.

Mundane & Technological Travel

Roads, rails, ferries, suborbital hops, and starliners. Reliable—if slower—and widely available in stable regions. Expect tolls, manifests, inspections, and the occasional shakedown in rough territories. Great for commerce, caravans, and moving inconspicuously under official eyes.

Common checks: Smarts: Technology (tickets, routes, maintenance), Smarts: Negotiation (booking, bribes), Nimbleness: Sneak (stowaway), Vibes: Observation (tails, traps).

Skyships & Realmships

Airborne craft ride steady winds—or brave the dreaded Gusts—between cities and even worlds. Realmships are reinforced to survive inter-realm currents and employ navigators who can read windshears, mana tides, and Shikai eddies. Passage is faster than roads, pricier than portals, and safer than raw ley-surfing—usually.

Common checks: Nimbleness: Pilot/Ride (helm), Smarts: Engineering (repairs), Vibes: Observation (weather), Ki: Kinesics (fine control in turbulence). Mods spike during storms, dogfights, or low-visibility.

Portals, Gates & Waylines

Ancient gates, keyed waylines, and temporary rifts allow near-instant traversal. Expect fees, keys, sigils, permissions, or alignment windows. Miskeyed exits risk disorientation or hostile territory. Stable city gates are heavily regulated; wild rifts appear where energies surge or reality thins.

Common checks: Aura: Presence (stabilize), Aura: Kinetics (shear dampening), Smarts: Lore (glyphs), Charm: Guile (permits). Resonance and Psyche often mitigate jump-sickness.

Ley-Line Sailing

Mana and Shikai channels form “currents” that seasoned navigators can ride on foot, in gliders, or aboard tuned craft. Fast and elegant, but volatile during storms or near contested nodes. Lines may fork; inexperienced crews can get swept into dangerous regions—or stranger places between.

Common checks: Smarts: Lore (maps & nodes), Ki: Kinesics (balance & flow), Aura: Kinetics (current shaping). Resonance helps read flows; Phase empowers bodily attunement.

Spiritual Transit

Astral projection, spirit walking, psychopomp caravans, and ancestor routes through the Umbra. Superb for reconnaissance, diplomacy, or bypassing physical blockades. Bodies must be guarded; tethers (anchors) must be maintained. Predatory entities stalk the unwary.

Common checks: Aura: Psionics (focus), Vibes: Attunement (safe paths), Toughness: Resolution (hold form). Psyche buffers mental strain; Resonance improves spirit-world clarity.

Using Travel at the Table (Hooks)

  • Time scales: Road days; skyship hours; portal moments; ley-line minutes (variable); spirit travel subjective.
  • Costs: Road (low), skyship (medium), realmship (high), portal (tolls/keys), ley sailing (gear/guide), spiritual (offerings/anchors).
  • Checks/Contests: Choose a leading Trait+Talent per mode; apply Mods for weather, regulations, crowds, Gusts, rift stability, etc.
  • Complications: Delays, inspections, storms, misalignment, predators, shortages, sabotage.
  • Power Factors: Spend Resonance to sense safe nodes; Phase for balance; Psyche to resist disorientation; Charge to overclock engines.
