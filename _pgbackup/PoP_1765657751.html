The Legacy Begins

Paths of Power began as a simple comic, a tale spun to entertain friends. Though fun and enjoyed in its time, the story's core essence lay dormant, its dusty remnants waiting decades for resurrection.

Now, that essence has been reborn. The original comic has been completely repurposed and adapted into a mature, expansive tabletop RPG setting, rich with enhanced lore and updated narratives. The foundation remains, but the world is deeper.

This is your moment. Embark on your own personal Path of Power.
