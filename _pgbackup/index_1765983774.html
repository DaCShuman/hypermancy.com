The Adventure of Creation

When this journey began, the goal was simple.
The dream was to create a role playing dice engine that truly allowed players to rise from
a nobody, to a sombody.
We wanted to be able to simulate a true rags to riches story, that feels right.
Start out as a random store associate who goes on to become a lead fisted heavy weight champion.
Play the role of survivors during a zombie apocolypse who rise up to save the world.
While the development process played out, we focused on a story focused experience system that rewards the most unique playstyles, just as much as it rewards combat oriented playstyles.
The Talent system aims to simulate real life capabilities, while uncapping truly insane potential.
This is where storytelling empowers players to truly explore the limits of their imagination.

Embrace A Universe of Realms

Paths of Power was once a comic I drew to entertain friends. It was fun and they enjoyed it, but then dark dusty remnants waited decades to be resurrected.
Today, my Paths of Power comic has been repurposed and adapted into a tabletop RPG setting. Much has been changed and enhanced to update the story into a more mature form, but the core essence remains.
Embark on your own personal Path of Power.

Discover a System Made by Gamers for Gamers

The Magnitude Dice System deploys an S3 system that gives simple quick instruction that seeks help new Storytellers move from mental ideas and possibilities, to fully drafted stories ready for the tabletop. The S3 system (Story, Scene, Situation) presents an easy to follow procedure that will help anyone tell that story they wish to deliver.
Our goal is to provide new storytellers with the equipment to tell whatever story you want to tell.
The MDS is intended to provide a tool kit that will bring your imagination to life, to entertain your friends or just to craft an epic story of your own.
