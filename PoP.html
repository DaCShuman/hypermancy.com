The Legacy Begins

A Living Universe Paths of Power is set in a sentient cosmos where every realm serves a vital function in the True Will. But for eons, sentient life has been forced to compete against malevolent entities and cosmic horrors just to survive.
The Long Silence For over 20,000 years, the Sol System was severed from this cosmic game. We were spared the galaxy's suffering only to be enslaved by hidden masters—corruptors who sought to break our psyche and turn humanity into obedient thralls. We lost our ancient knowledge, our history, and nearly our souls.
The Barrier Shatters In the planet's desperate death throes, Earth empowered a generation of heroes. Granting them incredible gifts, Gaia lashed out, shattering the barrier that imprisoned us. Now, the Sol System is reconnected to the Milky Way and beyond. Ancient pathways—the Weys and Hedges—are open once more, though they are fraught with forgotten dangers.
Your Path Begins Join the adventure as a seeker uncovering the truth of Earth's exile, or take command of a star vessel to smuggle and explore through the vast, lawless reaches of the Undead Sectors.
The choice is yours.
